Extra Credit

  • Our extra credit was implementing overlapping cells in the HOG feature extractor. Rectangular cells overlapped with each other by half, such that any non-border pixel contributed to the bins of 4 cells. Each pixel's contributions to its cells were weighted by a gaussian centered at the corresponding cells, with the cell size as sigma.
